This Saturday, Wallaroo Oval hosts Round 7 — and it's a big one.

**Kadina** arrive sitting first on the ladder and will be keen to keep it that way. They're a class side this year, but there's no better place to play finals-style football than at home in front of a packed outer.

**Bounce times — all grades:**

- **U9** — 9:00 AM
- **U11** — 9:00 AM
- **Junior Colts** — 10:00 AM
- **Senior Colts** — 11:20 AM
- **B Grade** — 12:55 PM
- **A-Grade** — 2:40 PM

## Sponsors Day & Auction

Round 7 is our **Sponsors Day** — the club's chance to acknowledge the businesses and individuals who make the Bulldogs possible. All 2026 sponsors are acknowledged at the ground.

The **annual auction** runs through the afternoon. Items include signed memorabilia, experiences and packages from our sponsor partners. All proceeds go directly to the club.

Get there early. The committee and volunteers will be working hard all day — come and support them.

---

Gates open from 8:30 AM. Full canteen and bar service throughout the day. See you at the oval.
